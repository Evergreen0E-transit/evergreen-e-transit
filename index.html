Why Evergreen?

We're not just another bus company. Evergreen is committed to zero emissions, affordable transportation, and long-term operational sustainability within Federal University Lokoja in Kogi State. Our electric buses offer quiet, reliable, and green mobility, solving the crisis of high transport costs and gasoline dependency in campus transport.

About Us

Evergreen Eagle E-Transit operates 30–50 seater luxury electric buses, specifically tailored for Nigerian university campuses. We integrate solar-supplemented charging grids for reduced operational costs. Our service ensures clean, affordable transport that endures – benefiting students, the university, and the environment.
