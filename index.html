About Us

Evergreen Eagle E-Transit is building a clean, affordable, and long-lasting electric bus system to serve students and staff of Federal University Lokoja. With zero emissions and luxurious 30–50 passenger capacity coaches, we are redefining sustainable campus mobility.

Why Evergreen?

  • 100% Zero-emission electric buses
  • Lower cost per trip than fuel-based transport
  • Durable, long-term service value
  • Smart AI-based route management (future integration)

Funding Goal

We’re currently raising between $1M–$1.5M USD to acquire 30–50 passenger luxury electric buses and fast-charging grid stations to power our launch at FUL.
